who we are?

Our Web Studio has its office based in Lviv, Ukraine. We have been operating since 2015 and currently employ staff located around the world. Our client list covers blue chip organisations through to significant high street and online retailers. Our solutions range from ecommerce websites through to back-end administrative and resource management, all solutions are web based and configured specifically to the client's requirements. When you appoint a web development company we understand it is essential you feel confident your chosen partner will realise your vision. At OurWebStudio.com our approach is symbiotic. We will work with you to understand your business and your end users requirements. We combine this understanding with our expertise & our proven processes, to maximise your websites potential. We understand your solution is an Investment and our role is to enable you to maximise your return on that investment. As with all Investments it is essential that you can accurately predict your costs. We provide a fixed price solution ensuring you avoid escalating development costs. For years we have perfected our expertise in designing and managing successful web solutions. We use This expertise to deliver, increased business and increased efficiencies for our clients. In our experience there is no such thing as an all-rounder when it comes to website design & development, therefore regardless of the size of a project we assign a team to ensure that each element is created by a specialist in their particular field. All our work is focused on producing targeted results for your business. Our project managers are all graduate calibre with a minimum of 2 years experience in industry and reputable IT, web, marketing and business qualifications. You don't have to take our word for it, we can provide customer testimonials and we are happy to arrange for any serious prospective clients to contact our existing clients to discuss what it is like to work with us.
